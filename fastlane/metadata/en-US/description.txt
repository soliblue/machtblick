Machtblick makes it clear how the German Bundestag decides.

Explore recorded votes, understand the results, and see how parliamentary groups and individual members voted. Clear visualizations, accessible summaries, and direct links to original sources help you follow parliamentary decisions.

With Machtblick, you can:

• browse and filter current and previous votes
• understand results and majorities at a glance
• review the voting record of individual members
• compare parliamentary groups by seats, attendance, cohesion, and positions
• trace motions and debates back to official documents
• save interesting votes and mark the ones you have read

The data comes from publicly available sources of the German Bundestag and other transparently identified sources. Automatically generated summaries are clearly marked and may contain errors.

Machtblick is independent, free, and ad-free. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no in-app purchases.
